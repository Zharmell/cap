Growth

  1. Matcha Guarding Game

    This project demonstrates my growth because I was shown an example of a gaurding game and instantly knew I wanted to do a matcha gaurding game, since people tend to say matcha tastes like grass. Majority of the time frame given to complete this game, designing it wasn't an issue but getting the score and lives to coordinate correctly was the hardest task. I struggled getting the cows to move at the right pace, and most importantly the score, but after seeing other examples from past students, I was able to complete it correctly!
